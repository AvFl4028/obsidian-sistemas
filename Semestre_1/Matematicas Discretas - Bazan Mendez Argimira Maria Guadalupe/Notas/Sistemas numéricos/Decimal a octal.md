### Decimal
$59.671875_{10}$

| Division entre 8 | residuo |
| ---------------- | ------- |
| $59 / 8 = 7$     | 3       |
| $7 / 8 = 7$      | 7       |
$59_{10}=73_{8}$
$0.671875*8=5.375$

$59.671875_{10}=73.53_{8}$
